Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how EventPin collects, uses, and discloses information when you use the EventPin mobile application ("App").

1. Information EventPin Collects

1.1 Location Information

When you use the App, EventPin may collect your device's location information to provide you with location-based features, such as showing your location on the map, loading events in your area, and re-centering the map to your location. Please note that EventPin does not store your location information on EventPin backend servers, and it is not shared with any third parties.

1.2 Advertisements

The App contains advertisements that are displayed to support the free availability of the App. These ads may be provided by third-party advertising networks or advertisers. Please be aware that these third parties may collect certain information about your interactions with the ads. This information is used for the purpose of delivering relevant ads and improving ad targeting. EventPin do not have access to or control over the data collected by third-party advertisers.

1.3 Photo Gallery Access

In order for you to select a profile photo, the App may require access to your photo gallery. When you grant permission, EventPin will only access the necessary photos to allow you to choose a profile photo. The selected photo will be saved on backend servers solely for the purpose of displaying your profile photo within the App. EventPin does not share your photo with any third parties.

1.4 Your email address

EventPin app also collects and saves your email address in the database upon sign-in. This is done to ensure smooth potential authentication provider migrations in the future and to potentially send you emails in case there are any important messages that need to be shared with you. Please be assured that your email address is treated confidentially and is not shared with any third parties

2. User Consent

2.1 Location and Photo Gallery Access

For both location and photo gallery access, the App will always request your permission before accessing these features. You have the option to grant or deny permission within the App's settings. If you choose to deny permission, certain features of the App may not function properly.

Please note that on iOS, the photo gallery widget operates independently of the app process, and the app itself does not have direct access to or visibility of the photos displayed in the widget. Access to photos is granted on a per-photo basis, meaning that when you select a specific photo, you are granting the app permission to access only that particular photo.

2.2 Push Notifications

The App provides push notifications to keep you updated on various events, including new followers, replies to your comments, new comments on events you created, updates on events you're attending, and new events created by users you follow. To send you push notifications, EventPin will ask for your permission. You have the flexibility to enable or disable push notifications in the App's settings.

3. Data Security

EventPin take reasonable measures to protect the information collected through the App from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, EventPin cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

4. Updates to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes to the Privacy Policy, we will notify you through the App or by other means. By continuing to use the App after the Privacy Policy has been updated, you automatically agree to the revised Privacy Policy.

If you do not agree with the updated Privacy Policy, please discontinue the use of the App.

5. Account deletion

5.1 Description

You can delete your account at any time. Please note that before deleting your account, you must cancel all active events associated with your account. Once you delete your account, all of your personal data will be removed from our system, except for the events you have created and the comments you have made.

In the event participants list, your profile image will be replaced with a placeholder and will not be tappable. In the event comments section, your username will be displayed as "[deleted]" and your profile picture will also be a placeholder, not tappable.

5.2 Steps

  1. Open the sidebar menu and tap on "My Profile" to open your User Profile page.
  2. In the top bar, you will see a 3-dot menu on the right. Tap on it.
  3. In the 3-dot menu, tap on "Delete Account".
  4. After tapping, a confirmation dialog will appear. Tap "Yes, delete my account".
  5. EventPin will attempt to delete your account. If you have created active events, you will be asked to first cancel them.
  6. After canceling all your active events, repeat steps 1, 2, 3, and 4.
  7. EventPin will start the deletion process. You will see a transparent loading screen saying "Deleting account...", in case the deletion process takes a longer time.
  8. Once the account deletion is complete, you will be logged out of your active session, and a success dialog confirming the account deletion will be shown.

6. Contact EventPin

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or EventPin's data practices, please contact EventPin at events@eventpin.net.

By using the EventPin App, you acknowledge and consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.
